# TabDen Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 20, 2026*

## Data collection

TabDen does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data (tab URLs, titles, and collection names) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.

## Data storage

All data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server, third party, or external service.

## Permissions

- `tabs` — to read open tab titles and URLs for saving
- `storage` — to save your collections locally
- `activeTab` — to read the current tab when using the popup
- `contextMenus` — to add a right-click save option

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL.
